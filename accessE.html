How to get to the Clinic

From Waikiki (or anywhere) to Liberty Ala Moana

By Taxi

When you are coming via taxi, make sure that the taxi will drop you off at Building 7 of our address! Waterfront Plaza has 7 buildings and is a very big area.

500 Ala Moana Blvd Building 7 Suite 302
Honolulu, HI, 96813

This building complex is known as WATERFRONT PLAZA, but most of the locals know it informally as "Restaurant Row." Your taxi driver may understand the latter better. Be sure to let the driver know to drop you off on the South St side.

If you are coming by Uber (or any other rideshare app), then searching for "Star Advertiser" would be the surest way to get to the right place. 

Once you make it to the Star Advertiser tower, go up the stairs and enter the lobby. If the door to the lobby is locked, please call the clinic (+1 808 566 6388) so that the staff can come down and help you!

Or just say these exact words to the driver.

"Please take me to the South St side of Restaurant Row (Waterfront Plaza), and drop me off in front of the stairs of the Star Advertiser Tower. Thank you."

Get on Ala Moana BLVD from Waikiki

Turn onto South St from Ala Moana Blvd

By Car

If you're driving yourself, please see the map above.

1

If coming from Waikiki, get on Ala Moana BLVD

Note: If you are coming from Ko Olina or anywhere on the west side, please drive on H-1E. Get off at Exit 18A (it will be to the right). Follow Nimitz Hwy all the way down, and it will become Ala Moana Blvd.

2

From Ala Moana Blvd, turn onto South St.

If you are coming from Waikiki, turn right on to South St. If you are coming from H-1, turn left onto South St.

3

Park in the structure on South St (left turn)

Take a ticket at the entrance, and we will provide validation. Once you come inside, park on the 2nd floor of the structure. Look for "BUILDING 7 LOBBY" written on the wall. The closer you park to the writing, the closer you will be to the clinic. Once you park, follow the arrow and enter the door to the STAR ADVERTISER TOWER (Building 7).

Getting to the Clinic

Once you make it to the Star Advertiser Tower (Building 7), then head towards the 3rd floor, Suite 302.

1.　

Once you enter the lobby for Building 7, head towards the 3rd floor.

2.

Once out of the elevator, you will see a dental clinic. Take a right.

3.

You will walk through a corridor filled with mirrors. This is correct.

4.

Once you pass the mirrors, you'll be in a hallway. Follow the left bend.

5.

You will be in an even longer hallway. We are the 2nd door on the left in here.

6.

Welcome to Liberty Dialysis Ala Moana Clinic. Come on in!
