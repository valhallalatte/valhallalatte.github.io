Welcome to Liberty Dialysis Ala Moana

The first dialysis clinic in Hawaii meant for travelers

A Clinic for Travelers

Our clinic opened in 2017 with a focus on travelers to Hawaii.
(JP ver) Because most of our visitors come from Japan, our staff are all bilingual in Japanese to make you feel at home.

15 minutes away from Waikiki

Our clinic is conveniently located approximately 15 minutes away from the heart of Waikiki, and approximately 7 minutes away from the famous Ala Moana Shopping Center.

Dialyze comfortably in our small clinic

Our small clinic offers a sense of privacy, so you can dialyze in peace in our comfortable reclining chairs.

TV and free WI-FI available

We offer complementary tv and wi-fi access for your entertainment. We also offer a high tech bidet toilet and other amenities as well.
(JP ver-we mention offering Japanese TV)

Feel at home in our new clinic

Our warm, genuine staff will greet you with Aloha when you walk into our new, clean clinic. Feel at home right from the moment you step inside.
